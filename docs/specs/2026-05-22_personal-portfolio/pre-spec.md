## 1. Overview

A personal, public-facing portfolio website for Erick Andrinirina, a Fullstack
JavaScript engineer with seven years of professional experience. The site
presents who he is, the technologies he masters, the companies he has worked
with, and the projects he has delivered. It serves as a living, online
complement to his CV and as a first impression for recruiters, clients, and
collaborators.

The visual design is already approved and will be the single source of truth
for layout, typography, color, and spacing. This document describes **what
content the site holds and how it behaves** — not how it looks pixel by pixel.

---

## 2. Objectives

1. Give visitors an immediate, credible sense of Erick's seniority and
   specialization (Fullstack JavaScript, 7 years).
2. Showcase the full breadth of his skills, employers, and projects in a way
   that is easy to scan and pleasant to read.
3. Let visitors reach him quickly through trusted, direct channels.
4. Be available in both English and French so it speaks to international and
   francophone audiences alike.
5. Be permanently online, fast, and reliable through free, low-maintenance
   hosting.
6. Protect private information (full home address) while still feeling personal
   and approachable.

## 6. How the site behaves

- **Language switching.** A clearly visible control lets visitors switch the
  entire interface and content between French and English. The site opens in
  French by default; the choice is remembered for the visit.
- **Theme.** The site honors the visitor's system light/dark preference on
  first load and offers a toggle to override it. The chosen theme is remembered.
- **CV download.** A "Download CV" action serves the PDF resume. *(The current
  CV is in French; an English version can be added later if desired.)*
- **Navigation.** Visitors can move quickly between the main sections (about,
  skills, experience, projects, contact) from anywhere on the page.
- **Responsiveness.** The layout reads well on phones, tablets, and desktops.
- **Performance & availability.** The site loads fast and is always online via
  GitHub Pages.
- **Discoverability.** The page carries a clear title, description, and social
  preview so that a shared link looks professional.
